The Illawarra Woodworkers Group has a very long and proud history of making and donating toys to local Illawarra Charities. Unfortunately we do not have a complete record or photographs of the fabulous work done in the early years, but we do have some from the past couple of years which are shown below.

## Past Donations

### Illawarra Prostate Foundation - November 11, 2009

Model Truck donated to Illawarra Prostate Foundation to raffle for fundraising, being accepted by Dennis Hamilton.

### Camp Quality - October 2009

Model B Double Truck donated to Camp Quality, Convoy for Kids.

### Wollongong Hospital Children's Ward - July 12, 2008

Toy train donated to Wollongong Hospital Children's Ward.
