We are located in the Illawarra region of New South Wales, about 80km South of Sydney, Australia. The major city of the Illawarra is Wollongong and the club is located in the suburb of Fairy Meadow. The club workshop is located in the grounds of Fairy Meadow Primary School. There is a small car park next to the club house or there is ample parking on the street.

## Directions by Car

From the Princes Highway turn into Balgownie Road and the entry gates are approx 150m on the left.
